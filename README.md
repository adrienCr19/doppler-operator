# The Doppler Operator Project

A research-notes website dedicated to the **boost operator** of relativistic Compton-scattering theory
(Chluba & Ravenni 2026; Chluba & Rosenberg 2026; Rosenberg & Chluba 2026; Hoey, Long & Chluba 2026) and to the
derivation of **new recursion relations for the Doppler operator**
`D^m_{ℓℓ'ℓ''}(ν,β) = γ⁻¹ · ⁻¹B(ν,−β) · ⁰B(ν,β)` — the round-trip scattering combination whose coefficient
structure the literature explicitly left as "a problem … for future work" (Rosenberg & Chluba 2026, §2.3).

**Main page:** `index.html` (GitHub Pages).

## Contents

- `index.html` — Section 1: complete catalog of boost-operator properties/identities/recurrences with
  equation-level citations. Section 2: the Doppler operator. Section 3: new results N1–N7.
  Section 4: symbolic tables generated by the new rapidity ODE. Section 5: verification log.
- `proofs/` — one self-contained proof page per new result:
  - `power-law-reduction.html` — Lemma L1 (operator ⇔ scalar-weight reduction)
  - `reflection.html` — **N1**: reflection symmetry `D(Ô) = Dᵀ(3−Ô)`; proves that diagonal elements are
    polynomials in the diffusion operator `D̂_ν = Ô² − 3Ô`
  - `parity.html` — **N2**: parity law `D(−β) = (−1)^{ℓ+ℓ''} D(β)`
  - `ode.html` — **N3**: closed rapidity ODE (the Doppler-operator generator)
  - `sumrule.html` — **N4**: sum rule over the scattering multipole (re-derivation, with attribution)
  - `raising.html` — **N5/N6**: outer-index raising recursions on the weight lattice
  - `seeds.html` — **N7**: seeds, a finite exact-generation algorithm, and a new ₂F₁ closed form for `D^m_mmm`
- `verification/` — Wolfram 15 (`wolframscript`) harnesses + saved PASS logs (`out_*.txt`):
  - `01_conventions.wl` — pins conventions; verifies literature identities (seeds, ℓ-raising, weight raising,
    parity, kernel ODE, inversion, closed form of D₀₀₀)
  - `02_doppler.wl` — verifies all new results N1–N6 + symbolic table generation and N1a invariance
  - `03_tables.wl` — ODE-generated series vs. closed forms and direct quadrature; reproduces the exact thermal
    SZ operator of Chluba & Rosenberg 2026 (Eq. 11) through O(p⁶)
  - `04_additivity.wl` — rapidity additivity
  - `05_export_tables.wl` — generates the tables of Section 4 (`doppler_tables_O.json`)

## Reproduce

```
cd verification
wolframscript -file 01_conventions.wl
wolframscript -file 02_doppler.wl
wolframscript -file 03_tables.wl
```

All exact identities pass at the 1e−27 level (30-digit quadrature); ODE checks are finite-difference limited
(~1e−12); truncated-sum checks are tail-limited (~1e−17).
