doppler_tables_p8.json — Doppler-operator elements to \(\mathcal O(p^8)\)

All nonzero Taylor coefficients of \(\hat{\mathcal D}^{m=0}_{\ell\ell'\ell''}(\nu,\beta)\) for channels \(\ell' = 0\ldots3\), outer indices \(\ell,\ell'' \le 6\), to eighth order in \(p = \gamma\beta\) — 501 entries generated by the verified rapidity ODE (N3). Format per entry:

{"l": 0, "lp": 2, "ldd": 0, "order": 4,
 "coeffs": ["0", "-12/45", "5/45", "6/45", "1/45"]}

meaning: the coefficient of \(p^{\rm order}\) in \(\hat{\mathcal D}_{\ell\,\ell'\,\ell''}\) equals \(\sum_j c_j\, q^j\), where \(q\) is the power-law exponent of the test spectrum \(\nu^q\) (operator form: substitute \(q \to -\hat O_\nu\)). Coefficients are exact rationals (strings). Verification: spot checks against the all-\(\beta\) closed forms (\(10^{-15}\), truncation-limited), the \(\hat{\mathcal S}_{\rm th}\) \(p^8\) coefficient against quadrature extrapolation, and exact reflection symmetry of every entry (17_tables_p8.wl). The \(p^2\)–\(p^6\) subsets reproduce [CR26, Eq. 11] and [RC26, Eqs. 9a,b].

doppler_tables_O.json — display tables (to \(\mathcal O(p^4)\), \(\hat O_\nu\) form)

The Section-4 tables of the main page as TeX strings in \(\hat O_\nu\) notation (channels \(\ell' = 0, 2\)).

Using the tables

Thermal averaging: replace \(p^k\) by the Maxwell–Jüttner moments \(\langle p^k\rangle = \frac{2(2\theta_{\rm e})^{k/2} K_{(k+4)/2}(1/\theta_{\rm e})\,\Gamma(\frac{k+3}{2})}{\sqrt{\pi}\,K_2(1/\theta_{\rm e})}\) [CR26, Eq. 13]; respect the superasymptotic truncation \(N_* \sim 1/\theta_{\rm e}\), or use the exact closed-form quadrature route (N8/N9) when \(\theta_{\rm e} \gtrsim 0.03\). Frequency-space action: \(q^j \to (x\partial_x)^j\) on the spectrum.
