N21 — The frame-exact kinematic \(C_k\), assembled from catalog elements

N16 proved that the physical kSZ correction operator cannot be assembled from diagonal Doppler elements (\(-q\,f(\Dv)\) obstruction) and left the frame-exact assembly as the follow-up. This page completes it. The key is the frame decomposition of [CR26, §3]: keep the photons isotropic (the CMB blackbody) and put all kinematics into (i) the boosted electron distribution, Legendre-decomposed with weights \(f_\ell \propto I_{\ell+1/2}(p_{\rm p}p/\theta_{\rm e})\), and (ii) the flux factor \(\tau^* = \tau/(1-\beta_{\rm p}\mu_{\rm p})\). The scattering operators are then the monopole-column elements — outer off-diagonal, hence exempt from the reflection constraint:

\[ \hat S_\ell(\nu,p) \;=\; \sqrt{2\ell+1}\,\Big[\Do_{\ell 0 0} + \tfrac1{10}\Do_{\ell 2 0}\Big] \;-\;\delta_{\ell 0}\;-\;\beta\,\delta_{\ell 1}, \]

and the dipole-channel signal per unit \(\tau^*\beta_{\rm p}\mu_{\rm p}\) is \(R^*(q,\theta_{\rm e}) = \frac{1}{3\theta_{\rm e}}\big\langle p\,\hat S_1\big\rangle\) (the \(\tau\)-convention adds the explicit flux term: \(R = R^* + \langle\hat S_0\rangle\)). Everything on the right is in this site's catalog: \(\Do_{100}, \Do_{120}\) have closed forms (bracket table), their series come from the N3 generator, and the Maxwell–Jüttner moments are exact Bessel ratios. harness 29_kinematic_ck.wl (W0–W4)

\[ R^* = -q\,\big(1 + \theta_{\rm e}\,\tilde C_1 + \dots\big),\qquad -q\,\tilde C_1 \;=\; -\Big[10\,x\partial_x + \tfrac{47}{5}x^2\partial_x^2 + \tfrac75 x^3\partial_x^3\Big], \]
