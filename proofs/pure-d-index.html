N25 — Raising every index with Doppler operators only

Goal. Give explicit recurrence relations that raise each index of the Doppler operator \(\Do_{\ell\ell'\ell''}(q)\) in which every object that appears is itself a physical Doppler operator — no aberration kernels \(\Kk\), no boost operators \(\Bo\), no bracket \(F\), and in particular no extended off-diagonal family \(X\). The whole catalogue is generated from the single seed \(\Do_{000}\) by \(\Do\)-only moves. The hard case — the shared middle index \(\ell'\) — is the centrepiece (§3); the outer indices (§2) and the two structural fillers (§4) are collected around it.

0  Notation

Rapidity \(\eta\) with \(\gamma=\cosh\eta\), \(p=\gamma\beta=\sinh\eta\); the universal functions and coefficients

\[ J_w \;=\; \frac{(\gamma+p)^w-(\gamma-p)^w}{w} \;=\; \frac{2\sinh(w\eta)}{w}\quad(J_0=2\eta,\ J_{-w}=J_w), \qquad C_\ell \;=\; \sqrt{\frac{\ell^2}{4\ell^2-1}} \;=\; \frac{\ell}{\sqrt{4\ell^2-1}} . \]

The only base case is the seed closed form

\[ \Do_{000}(q) \;=\; \frac{J_{2+q}\,J_{1+q}}{4\gamma p^2}. \]

Two border families carry the recursion: the axis elements \(\Do_{a00}\), \(\Do_{00b}\) (one nonzero index) and the \(\ell''{=}0\) border \(\Do_{h\ell'0}\). Everything else is filled algebraically. Spectral-index shifts \(q\to q\pm1\) are allowed: \(\Do(q\pm1)\) is still a physical Doppler operator.

1  The one fact everything rests on: factorization + a \(\Do\)-dictionary

On power laws the Doppler operator is a product of two kernels sharing the scattering index \(\ell'\) (Lemma L1),

\[ \Do_{\ell\ell'\ell''}(q) \;=\; \frac1\gamma\,\underbrace{{}^{-1-q}\Kk_{\ell\ell'}(+\beta)}_{\textstyle F_{\ell\ell'}(q)}\; \underbrace{{}^{-q}\Kk_{\ell'\ell''}(-\beta)}_{\textstyle G_{\ell'\ell''}(q)} . \]

Two elementary identities turn each kernel factor back into a physical \(\Do\):

  • Parity (N2): \(G_{b0}(q)={}^{-q}\Kk_{b0}(-\beta)=(-1)^{b}\,{}^{-q}\Kk_{b0}(+\beta)=(-1)^b F_{b0}(q-1)\);
  • Seed value: \(G_{00}(q-1)={}^{-(q-1)}\Kk_{00}(-\beta)=J_{q}/(2p)\), so \(\Do_{b00}(q-1)=\tfrac1\gamma F_{b0}(q-1)\,J_q/(2p)\).

Eliminating \(F_{b0}(q-1)\) between them, and using \(\Do_{ab0}(q)=\tfrac1\gamma F_{ab}(q)G_{b0}(q)\), gives the dictionary — a kernel factor as a ratio of physical elements:

\[ \boxed{\;F_{ab}(q) \;=\; (-1)^b\,\frac{J_q}{2p}\,\frac{\Do_{ab0}(q)}{\Do_{b00}(q-1)}\;} \tag{D} \]

check PP5/P3a, verified to 10⁻²² This is the device that removes every kernel from the recursions below.

2  Outer indices — the axis ladders (P0)

Along the axes the weight companion produced by the boost recursion is absorbed into a physical \(\Do\) at a shifted \(q\), because \(G_{00}\) has the explicit value above. The result is a genuine three-term ladder in physical elements:

\[ \textbf{(P0}_\ell\textbf{)}\qquad C_a\,\Do_{a00}(q) \;=\; \frac{\gamma}{p}\,\Do_{a-1,00}(q)\;-\;\frac1p\,\frac{J_{1+q}}{J_{2+q}}\,\Do_{a-1,00}(q{+}1) \;-\; C_{a-1}\,\Do_{a-2,00}(q), \] \[ \textbf{(P0}_{\ell''}\textbf{)}\qquad C_b\,\Do_{00b}(q) \;=\; \frac{\gamma}{p}\,\Do_{0,0,b-1}(q)\;-\;\frac1p\,\frac{J_{2+q}}{J_{1+q}}\,\Do_{0,0,b-1}(q{-}1) \;-\; C_{b-1}\,\Do_{0,0,b-2}(q). \]

These build the two outer axes from \(\Do_{000}\) alone. check P0a/P0b, \(\le5\) rungs, \(q\in\{\tfrac12,-\tfrac{7}{10}\}\), dev 3×10⁻³⁷

3  The middle index \(\ell'\) — the centrepiece (P3)

The shared index is the hard one: a first-order move acts on both factors \(F_{\ell\ell'}\) and \(G_{\ell'\ell''}\) at once, so a linear \(\ell'\)-relation cannot close on the diagonal (N10 needs the off-diagonal family \(X\)). Working on the \(\ell''{=}0\) border and paying with a ratio removes that obstruction entirely and keeps every object a physical \(\Do\).

3.1  Derivation

Step 1 — column ladder for leg 1. The verified column recursion of the \(+\beta\) kernel (N10, Ingredient A) raises the shared index of the first factor:

\[ F_{h,\ell'+1}(q) \;=\; -\frac{1}{C_{\ell'+1}\,p}\Big[\gamma\,F_{h\ell'}(q) - F_{h\ell'}(q{-}1)\Big] \;-\; \frac{C_{\ell'}}{C_{\ell'+1}}\,F_{h,\ell'-1}(q). \tag{A} \]

Step 2 — substitute the dictionary (D) for each of the four brackets. Writing (D) at \((a,b)=(h,\ell'+1),(h,\ell'),(h,\ell'),(h,\ell'-1)\) with arguments \(q,q,q-1,q\), the common factor \((-1)^{\ell'}J_q/2p\) — note \((-1)^{\ell'+1}\) and the \(J_{q-1}\) of the third bracket — cancels after dividing through, leaving a relation among physical elements only. Collecting the axis denominators \(\Do_{\ell'00}\) and clearing \(C_{\ell'+1}\) gives the boxed result.

(P3)  middle-index raising, pure \(\Do\) new verified 1.3×10⁻³⁷
\[ \boxed{ \begin{aligned} C_{\ell'+1}\,\Do_{h,\ell'+1,0}(q) \;=\;& \frac{\Do_{\ell'+1,00}(q{-}1)}{p} \left[\,\gamma\,\frac{\Do_{h\ell'0}(q)}{\Do_{\ell'00}(q{-}1)} \;-\; \frac{J_{q-1}}{J_q}\,\frac{\Do_{h\ell'0}(q{-}1)}{\Do_{\ell'00}(q{-}2)}\,\right] \\[4pt] &-\; C_{\ell'}\,\frac{\Do_{\ell'+1,00}(q{-}1)}{\Do_{\ell'-1,00}(q{-}1)}\;\Do_{h,\ell'-1,0}(q). \end{aligned}} \tag{P3} \]

Read off the ingredients: two border elements one middle-level down, \(\Do_{h\ell'0}(q)\) and \(\Do_{h\ell'0}(q{-}1)\); one two levels down, \(\Do_{h,\ell'-1,0}(q)\); and four axis elements \(\Do_{\ell'+1,00}(q{-}1),\ \Do_{\ell'00}(q{-}1),\ \Do_{\ell'00}(q{-}2),\ \Do_{\ell'-1,00}(q{-}1)\), all supplied by (P0). The lone scalar transcendental is \(J_{q-1}/J_q=\dfrac{q\,\sinh((q-1)\eta)}{(q-1)\sinh(q\eta)}\). The outer index \(h\) rides along untouched, so (P3) raises \(\ell'\) at every fixed \(h\) at once.

3.2  The first two steps, explicitly

Monopole → dipole \((\ell'{=}0,\ C_1=1/\sqrt3\); the \(C_0=0\) term drops):

\[ \frac{1}{\sqrt3}\,\Do_{h10}(q) \;=\; \frac{\Do_{100}(q{-}1)}{p} \left[\gamma\,\frac{\Do_{h00}(q)}{\Do_{000}(q{-}1)} - \frac{J_{q-1}}{J_q}\,\frac{\Do_{h00}(q{-}1)}{\Do_{000}(q{-}2)}\right]. \]

Dipole → quadrupole \((\ell'{=}1,\ C_2=2/\sqrt{15})\):

\[ \frac{2}{\sqrt{15}}\,\Do_{h20}(q) \;=\; \frac{\Do_{200}(q{-}1)}{p} \left[\gamma\,\frac{\Do_{h10}(q)}{\Do_{100}(q{-}1)} - \frac{J_{q-1}}{J_q}\,\frac{\Do_{h10}(q{-}1)}{\Do_{100}(q{-}2)}\right] \;-\; \frac{1}{\sqrt3}\,\frac{\Do_{200}(q{-}1)}{\Do_{000}(q{-}1)}\,\Do_{h00}(q). \]

Iterating from \(h{=}0\) produces the whole \(\ell''{=}0\) border \(\{\Do_{h\ell'0}\}\); each level is built from the two below it and the axis, with no external input. check GENb: the self-fed tower \(\Do_{h\ell'0}\), \(h\le3,\ \ell'\le4\), dev 1.4×10⁻³⁸

3.3  Why the ratio is unavoidable (and harmless)

A linear diagonal \(\ell'\)-relation cannot exist: stepping the shared index multiplies two three-term recursions and unavoidably generates mismatched \((\ell'{-}1,\ell'{-}2)\) pairings — the off-diagonal companions of N10. (P3) trades that linear-but-extended form for a nonlinear-but-pure one: the companions are absorbed into the ratios \(\Do_{h\ell'0}/\Do_{\ell'00}\), which are exactly the leg-1 factors \(F\) restored by the dictionary (D). Nothing outside the physical family ever appears.

4  Closing the plane — reflection (N1) and rank-one fill (P2)

(P3) delivers the \(\ell''{=}0\) border. The mirror border and the interior are algebraic:

\[ \textbf{(N1)}\qquad \Do_{h\,\ell'\,l}(q) \;=\; \Do_{l\,\ell'\,h}(-3-q) \qquad\Longrightarrow\qquad \Do_{0\ell'l}(q)=\Do_{l\ell'0}(-3-q), \] \[ \textbf{(P2)}\qquad \Do_{h\,\ell'\,l}(q) \;=\; \frac{\Do_{h\ell'0}(q)\;\Do_{0\ell'l}(q)}{\Do_{0\ell'0}(q)} . \]

(N1) is the reflection symmetry (N1): it turns the border produced by (P3) into the opposite border at reflected spectral index. (P2) is the rank-one property of the factorization (\(\Do_{h\ell'l}=\tfrac1\gamma F_{h\ell'}G_{\ell'l}\) is a product of an \(h\)-vector and an \(l\)-vector), so any fixed-\(\ell'\) plane equals the outer product of its two borders divided by the corner. check N1 (3×10⁻³⁶), P2 (exact)

5  End-to-end

Putting §2–§4 together generates any element from the seed with physical Doppler operators at every step. Two independent deep targets, built by (P0)→(P3)→(N1)→(P2) with no kernel formed anywhere:

\[ \Do_{2,3,2}\big(\tfrac12\big),\qquad \Do_{3,2,1}\big(-\tfrac{7}{10}\big) \qquad\text{— reproduced exactly (dev }=0\text{).} \]

The middle index, once the awkward spectator of N3/N5/N6 and the reason N10 needed the family \(X\), now has a self-contained \(\Do\)-only raising law. Combined with the outer ladders and the two fillers, the entire generation calculus closes on the physical Doppler operator alone.
