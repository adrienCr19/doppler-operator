Generating Doppler operators from Doppler operators

The goal

The point of the whole programme is to compute a new Doppler operator by recurrence from ones already computed — or from the single seed \(\Do_{000}\) — without ever multiplying two boost / aberration kernels together. This page collects the relations that achieve exactly that, at three levels of strength. The strongest — the pure-physical scheme (P0–P4 of the generation paper) — generates any element with every intermediate a genuine physical Doppler operator, verified end-to-end (from \(\Do_{000}\) to interior elements, no kernels anywhere). All boxed relations are checked in verification/22_pure_physical.wl and 20_reconcile_generation.wl (\(s=0\), \(m=0\); \(J_w = \frac{(\gamma+p)^w-(\gamma-p)^w}{w}\), \(C_\ell = \ell/\sqrt{4\ell^2-1}\)).

1  The pure-physical generation scheme (every term a Doppler operator)

The seed and four moves. Nothing below is a kernel; each relation is a recurrence among physical \(\Do\)'s at spectral arguments \(q, q\pm1\).

Seed \(\Do_{000}\)
\[ \Do_{000}(q) = \frac{J_{2+q}\,J_{1+q}}{4\gamma p^2}. \]
(P0) Axis ladders — build the two outer axes from \(\Do_{000}\) PP1/PP2, exact
\[ C_{a}\,\Do_{a00}(q) = \frac{\gamma}{p}\,\Do_{a-1,0,0}(q) - \frac{1}{p}\frac{J_{1+q}}{J_{2+q}}\,\Do_{a-1,0,0}(q{+}1) - C_{a-1}\,\Do_{a-2,0,0}(q), \]

and the mirror ladder for \(\Do_{00b}(q)\) (with \(J_{2+q}/J_{1+q}\) and the shift \(q\to q{-}1\)). Seeded directly by \(\Do_{000}\): \(C_1\Do_{100}(q) = \tfrac\gamma p\Do_{000}(q) - \tfrac1p\tfrac{J_{1+q}}{J_{2+q}}\Do_{000}(q{+}1)\). One-index, linear, purely physical.

(P2) Rank-one — fill any fixed-\(\ell'\) plane from one row and one column PP3, exact
\[ \Do_{h\ell'l}(q) = \frac{\Do_{h\ell'0}(q)\,\Do_{0\ell'l}(q)}{\Do_{0\ell'0}(q)}. \]

At fixed \(\ell'\) the array of physical elements is rank one; the border (\(l=0\) row and \(h=0\) column) determines the interior. For \(\ell'=0\) this and P0 already generate the entire plane from \(\Do_{000}\).

(N1) Reflection — one border from the other verified
\[ \Do_{\ell\ell'\ell''}(q) = \Do_{\ell''\ell'\ell}(-3-q) \quad\Rightarrow\quad \Do_{0\ell'l}(q) = \Do_{l\ell'0}(-3-q). \]
(P3) Middle-index step — climb \(\ell'\) along the border PP5/PP6

The one move that changes the shared scattering multipole. It is necessarily nonlinear (a ratio of physical elements): a shift of the shared index moves both scattering legs, and the diagonal slice is codimension one, so no linear physical recurrence in \(\ell'\) can exist — the rank-one structure (P2) is exactly what survives. Its ingredient, the bracket-as-ratio, is physical (PP5): \(\ (-1)^{b}J_{q}\,\Do_{ab0}(q) = 2p\,\Do_{b00}(q{-}1)\cdot[\text{bracket}]\); substituting it into the two border ladders gives \(\Do_{h,\ell'+1,0}(q)\) as a (nine-term, nonlinear) combination of \(\Do_{h,\ell',0}, \Do_{h,\ell'-1,0}\) at \(q, q{-}1\) with coefficients built from the axis elements \(\Do_{\ell'00}, \Do_{\ell'-1,0,0}\). Verified to close on physical \(\Do\)'s (PP6).

The scheme (Corollary P4) — and a verified end-to-end run PP4, exact

To generate any \(\Do_{\ell\ell'\ell''}\) from \(\Do_{000}\): (i) P0 builds the axes \(\Do_{a00}, \Do_{00b}\); (ii) P3 climbs the middle index to the border \(\Do_{h\ell'0}\); (iii) N1 reflects it to \(\Do_{0\ell'l}\); (iv) P2 fills the interior. Every intermediate is a physical Doppler operator. Demonstrated end-to-end (PP4): the interior element \(\Do_{303}\) was generated from \(\Do_{000}\) by P0 (axes \(\Do_{300}, \Do_{003}\)) and P2 (rank-one) — no kernel ever formed — matching the direct value exactly.

2  The outer-pair relation (P1) — a single pure-physical recurrence

(P1) R3, \(10^{-27}\)
\[ C_{\ell+1}\Do_{\ell+1,\ell'\ell''}(q) + C_\ell\Do_{\ell-1,\ell'\ell''}(q) - C_{\ell''+1}\Do_{\ell,\ell',\ell''+1}(q{+}1) - C_{\ell''}\Do_{\ell,\ell',\ell''-1}(q{+}1) = \frac{\gamma}{p}\big[\Do_{\ell\ell'\ell''}(q) - \Do_{\ell\ell'\ell''}(q{+}1)\big]. \]

Both outer indices in one relation, every term physical — the companion of the single-index ladders (N5/N6) has been eliminated by the gauge identity \({}^{(j+1,k+1)}X(q) = {}^{(j,k)}X(q+1)\), which trades a weight-lattice companion for a physical element at shifted \(q\). Self-reflected under N1.

3  The other Doppler-level generators

  • The rapidity ODE (N3) — continuous. \(\partial_\eta \Do_{\ell\ell'\ell''}\) is a closed combination of \(\Do_{\ell\pm1,\ell'\ell''}\) and \(\Do_{\ell\ell'\ell''\pm1}\) at fixed \(\ell'\) — the differential generator of the whole family, again with no kernels. Integrate from \(\Do|_{\eta=0}=\delta\) for the \(p\)-series.
  • The lattice recurrences (N5/N6, N10). Raise outer or middle indices with generalized Doppler companions on the weight lattice \({}^{(j,k)}\Do\) (themselves Doppler-type operators, not kernels). Slightly more general than the pure-physical relations, at the cost of the auxiliary family. P0/P1 are what these become once the gauge identity removes the lattice.

4  Where these come from (origin, not a tool)

The recurrences exist because the Doppler operator factorizes, on power laws, into two aberration-kernel columns — \(\Do_{\ell\ell'\ell''}(q) = \tfrac{(-1)^{\ell'+\ell''}}{\gamma}\,{}^{-1-q}\Kk_{\ell\ell'}(+\beta)\,{}^{-q}\Kk_{\ell'\ell''}(-\beta)\) (the definition; see the reconciliation note). Each recurrence above is the kernel’s own \(\ell\)-raising relation, pushed through this factorization and re-expressed among physical \(\Do\)'s. But the factorization is not a generation tool: using it means multiplying two kernels — the very step every relation on this page is built to avoid. It explains why the \(\Do\)-recurrences exist and why the closed forms factorize; the recurrences are the deliverable.
